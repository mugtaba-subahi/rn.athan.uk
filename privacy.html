Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Athan: London is committed to protecting your privacy. We want to make it clear that we do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our users. The application operates by retrieving prayer time information from a reliable external source and presenting this data to the user. No user information is gathered or retained during this process.

Permissions

Our application has been designed with minimal permissions in mind. It does not require access to sensitive device features such as location services, camera, microphone, or any other personal data. The core functionality is limited to displaying prayer times and providing timely notifications, ensuring maximum privacy for our users.

Notifications

The notification system in Athan: London is designed solely to inform users about upcoming prayer times. These alerts are generated based on the prayer time data we retrieve and do not involve any form of user tracking or data collection. Users have full control over notification preferences within the application settings.

Transparency

We maintain a policy of complete transparency regarding our data practices. Should you have any questions or require further clarification about our privacy measures, we encourage you to reach out to us through our official support channels. Your trust is important to us, and we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy protection.
